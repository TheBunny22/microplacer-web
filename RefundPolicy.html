    You may submit a cancellation request by contacting our customer support team or through your user account on our website. Please provide all the necessary details related to your order or subscription for faster processing.

  • 2. Cancellation Deadlines

    The cancellation deadline for your order or subscription may vary depending on the specific product or service. Please refer to the terms and conditions provided during the purchase or subscription process for the applicable cancellation deadline.

  • 3. Refunds

    If your cancellation request is received within the specified cancellation deadline, we will process the refund according to our Refund Policy (see below). Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase, unless otherwise agreed upon.

Refund Policy

The refund will be processed within [number of days] and issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.     • c. Customized or Personalized Products: Products that have been customized or personalized according to your specific requirements may not be eligible for refunds.
