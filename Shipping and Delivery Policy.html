Shipping and Delivery Policy

1. Order Processing:

Once your PCB order is confirmed and payment is received, our team begins processing it promptly. We review the order details, including PCB specifications, quantity, and any additional requirements. If there are any issues or clarifications needed, our customer support team will reach out to you for further assistance.

2. Manufacturing and Quality Assurance:

Our highly skilled manufacturing team works diligently to produce your PCBs with precision and quality. We adhere to strict quality control standards throughout the manufacturing process to ensure the highest level of reliability and performance. In case of any manufacturing delays or issues, we will notify you promptly and provide alternative solutions.

3. Packaging:

Once your PCBs are ready, we carefully package them to prevent any damage during transit. We use industry-standard packaging materials, including anti-static bags and protective padding, to safeguard your PCBs.

4. Shipping Methods and Carriers:

We offer multiple shipping methods to accommodate your needs, including standard, expedited, and international shipping options. We work with reputable shipping carriers to ensure reliable and timely delivery of your PCBs. You can select your preferred shipping method during the checkout process.

5. Shipping Timeframes:

Our Standard time for order Complition is in between 7-10 Days, And Curiur time will be 1-2 Days depends on customer location. Over all Dilivery time for all order is 10-11 Working Days.

6. Tracking and Notifications:

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number or link to track the progress of your shipment. You will receive notifications via email or SMS regarding the status of your shipment, including any significant updates or delays.

7. Customs and Duties:

For international shipments, please be aware that customs procedures and duties may apply. Any customs fees or import duties imposed by the destination country are the responsibility of the customer. We cannot predict or control these charges, and they are not included in the product or shipping prices.

8. Delivery and Receipt:

It is important to provide accurate shipping information, including the correct recipient name, address, and contact details. Please ensure someone is available to receive the delivery at the provided address. If no one is available, the carrier may attempt redelivery or leave a notice for further instructions.

9. Damaged or Lost Shipments:

In the rare event that your shipment arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact our customer support immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.

10. Returns and Refunds:

Please refer to our Returns and Refunds Policy for detailed information on returning products and initiating refunds.

We are committed to providing excellent customer service and ensuring your satisfaction throughout the shipping and delivery process. If you have any questions or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team. Thank you for choosing Micoplacer for your PCB manufacturing needs.
